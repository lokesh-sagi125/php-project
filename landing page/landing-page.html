What do we do?
Crowd management solutions are tools and strategies designed to efficiently control and organize the movement and behavior of crowds in various places such as events, hubs, mall, restaurants and more. It aims to enhance safety, security, and the overall experience for both event organizers and attendees. Below graph shows the increase in the number of population in the world
How do we do it?
Our application takes input from CCTV footage, processes the footage in the client's computer using YOLOV8 as the neural network which identifies a human head and when it crosses a preset margin line it adds to the total crowd count. Then it updates the information and provides the user with the current crowd data.
